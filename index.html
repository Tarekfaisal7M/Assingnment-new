This summer, our own Ashley Junger traveled to the spectacularly mountainous Reserva Ecológica de Guapiaçu (REGUA) to take part in the expedition Wildlife and Reforestation in Brazil. She surveyed small mammals in an old-growth forest with Dr. Manoel Muanis and other Earthwatch volunteers.
